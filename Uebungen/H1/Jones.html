Fried Chicken Recipe

Instructions to make the perfectly seasoned Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon Paprika
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 Teaspoon black peper
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together eggs and milk.
  3. Dip each piece of chicken in the egg mixture, then dredge in the flour mixture, ensuring it is fully coated.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  5. Fry chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 15 minutes per batch.
  6. Drain on paper towels.

Enjoy your delicious Homemade fried Chicken!
